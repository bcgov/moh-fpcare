Request Confirmation of your Fair PharmaCare Assistance

To have a copy of your Confirmation of Fair PharmaCare Assistance mailed to you, complete the information below and select the Request Confirmation button. Please allow two to three weeks for delivery.

If you recently applied and your income has not yet been verified with the Canada Revenue Agency, you will not receive a Confirmation of Assistance letter until your income has been verified. To see your level of temporary assistance, enter the income you reported for you and your spouse (if applicable) in the Fair PharmaCare Calculator.
