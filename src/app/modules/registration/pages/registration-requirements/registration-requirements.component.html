Register for Fair PharmaCare

What you need:

To complete your registration online, you will need to provide information about you (registrant), your family, and your spouse/common-law partner.

Family

For each member of your family (including your children) you will need to provide their:
  • Personal Health Number (PHN)
  • Date of Birth

Note: If you share custody of any of your children with a former partner, the children can only be on one parent's Fair PharmaCare account.

Registrant and Spouse/Common-law Partner

For you and your spouse/common-law partner, you will also need to provide your:
  • Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • Net Income (Line 23600) from the income tax return or from the Notice of Assessment or Reassessment from two years ago.
    Example: If you are registering in {{benefitYearEx}}, use the {{taxYearEx}} income tax return information; If you are registering in {{benefitYearEx + 1}}, use the {{taxYearEx + 1}} income tax return information.
  • If applicable, the amount of any income from a Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP) from Line 12500 of the Income Tax Return from two years ago.
  • Written consent to verify your income(s) with the Canada Revenue Agency.
Please do not use this online registration system if:
  • You have registered for Fair PharmaCare in the past
  • You need to add family members to your Fair PharmaCare account
  • You need to remove family members from your Fair PharmaCare account.

Please contact Health Insurance BC to restore coverage, or update family information.

Each screen has a navigation bar. Use these buttons to move forward or backward through the screens.

There are seven to eight screens in this application. The whole process should take less than 30 minutes. It must be completed in a single session. Information will not be stored until your application is submitted.
