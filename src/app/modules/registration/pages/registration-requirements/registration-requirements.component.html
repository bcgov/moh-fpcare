Register for Fair PharmaCare

What you need:

To complete your registration online, you will need to provide information about you (registrant), your family, and your spouse. You must use a compatible web browser.

Application

For each person in your application, you will need to give us their:
  • Personal Health Number
  • Date of Birth

Registrant and Spouse

For you and your spouse, you will also need to give use your:
  • Social Insurance Number
  • Net Income (Line 236) from your Tax Return or from your Notice of Assessment from two years ago.
    Example: If you are registering in 2019, use your 2017 tax return information; If you are registering in 2020, use your 2018 tax return information.
  • If applicable, the amount of any income from a Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP) from Line 125 of your Income Tax Return from two years ago.
